Hi, I'm Qicong (Martin) Jin.

Mechatronics Engineering Student @ UoA

I have always found it difficult to separate my interests into just one bucket. Whether it was building physical mechanisms, designing circuits, or writing logic to control them, I realized that my best work happened when all three disciplines overlapped. I chose Mechatronics because it combines mechanical, electrical, and computer systems into a single cohesive field, allowing me to be exposed to the full range of engineering challenges. In my experience, the most complex engineering challenges are rarely solved within a single domain. I enjoy multidisciplinary problem-solving because it allows me to maintain a "big-picture" view of a project—ensuring that the hardware is optimized for the electronics, and the software is tuned perfectly to the physical constraints of the system.

About this site: This website is a gallery of my engineering journey. It serves to document my technical growth and showcase how I bridge the gap between theoretical analysis and physical implementation. Note it is still a work in progress, and texts may not be updated for all projects.
